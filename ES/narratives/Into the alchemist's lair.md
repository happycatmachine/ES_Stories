
## tiles

### a - entrance

South chest hides a secret, after unlocking


### b - skeleees

All hexes spawn [[Skeleton]], use white chips to represent them.

On skeleton death, leave bones (white chips). These are not obstacles.

If any party members occupy a boned hex after all [[Skeleton]] have been defeated, they gain 2 fatigue (all on boned hexes). All unoccupied hexes spawn [[Skeleton]].

Have fun!


### c - pit trap

centre hex is a trap, spot it by rolling low on two dice.

if not spotted, all other players roll a d6, highest roll is saving roll.

How are you going to save...?

[[Wisp]] spawns in centre of room.

Spikes at the bottom glow with a sickly green hue, as if pulsing with some kind of necrotic energy. A subtle stench of rotten meat, you can make out most decomposed skeletal remains of previous adventurers, or plunderers.
