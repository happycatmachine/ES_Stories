# Arise from the ash

A clatter of bones and ash hit the ground, amongst the debris is a pendant...falling, roll a d6

The pendant hits the ground with a crash, its gem breaks and explodes, the shards sucked in as if opening a gate, it widens and stepping through is a [[Skeleton Mage]].
